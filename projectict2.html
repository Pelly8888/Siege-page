Top 5 Attackers in Rainbow Six Siege

  1. Ash – Fast entry and strong breaching.
  2. Sledge – Great for soft breaching and vertical play.
  3. Thermite – Key hard breacher for team plays.
  4. Iana – Useful clone drone for gathering intel.
  5. Jackal – Tracks enemy footprints easily.
